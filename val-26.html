I know we’re far apart,
but being with you still makes me feel close. Every day is just another day until our next vacation together.
I won’t lie and say I can’t live without you — I can — but life wouldn’t feel the same. The little things would lose their color; the world would still be loud and chaotic, but everything in it would feel quieter inside me.
You make me feel whole. You lift me when I’m at my lowest. Your presence, even through a screen, feels like a warm hug — even if it’s just a small smooch from our late-night video calls.
Thank you for being in my life, for choosing this relationship with me, and for giving me the chance to prove, again and again, that you mean the world to me.
I love you. ❤️
